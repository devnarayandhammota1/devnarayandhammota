Our Core Mission

Transforming Lives:
We are dedicated to eliminating social challenges such as child labor, bonded labor, addiction, and illiteracy, aiming for a just and fair society. Protecting Nature: Our initiatives focus on environmental conservation and safeguarding Rajasthan’s unique biodiversity and wildlife.

Spreading Knowledge:
Believing in education as a powerful tool for change.Preserving Culture: We work to uphold traditional values and heritage, fostering pride and identity in local communities.

Building Brighter Futures Together

Educational Support through Scholarships

We offer financial aid to children in underserved rural areas, with a special focus on girls, helping them gain access to quality education and empowering them to build brighter futures.

Rural Health & Wellness Program

We conduct free medical camps in remote communities, delivering essential healthcare services and raising awareness about preventive health practices to foster overall well-being.

Women Empowerment & Skill Development

We provide vocational training and skill-building workshops for women in rural areas, enabling them to become financially independent and active contributors to their communities..

Clean Water & Sanitation Program

We work to provide access to clean drinking water and improved sanitation facilities in rural communities, promoting hygiene and reducing waterborne diseases for healthier living conditions.

Digital Literacy & Skill Development

We provide digital literacy training and access to technology for rural youth and women, empowering them with essential skills for education,andentrepreneurship in the modern world.

Eco-Friendly Farming Initiative

We assist local farmers in adopting eco-conscious farming methods, encouraging practices that enhance soil fertility,and boost crop productivity for sustainable agricultural development.

Enviro Awareness & Conservation

We promote environmental education and community-led conservation efforts to protect natural resources, reduce pollution, and foster a culture of sustainability in rural areas

Women Empowerment & Livelihood

We empower rural women through self-help groups, vocational training, and micro-enterprises to achieve financial independence and drive positive change in their communities.

Youth Leadership & Life Skills Program

We engage rural youth in leadership training, soft skills development, and community service activities, nurturing responsible citizens and future leaders.

Disaster Preparedness & Relief Support

We provide emergency relief during natural calamities and educate communities on disaster preparedness, helping them respond effectively and recover more quickly.

Child Nutrition & Midday Meal Program

We support nutrition programs in schools and communities to ensure children receive balanced meals, which improves their health, learning ability, and school attendance.

Senior Citizen Care & Inclusion

We conduct outreach for elderly community members through wellness camps, social support groups, and access to essential services, ensuring they live with dignity and care.

Empowering Communities for a Better Tomorrow

Shri Devnarayan Mandir Trust is a spiritual and cultural organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage and teachings of Lord Devnarayan. The trust manages the temple activities, organizes religious festivals, and supports various community welfare initiatives. It plays a vital role in maintaining the sanctity of the temple premises, facilitating pilgrim services, and encouraging devotion among followers.
