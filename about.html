Our Purpose & Dream

Our Purpose

At Shri Devnarayan Temple Trust, we strive to inspire inclusive growth by intertwining cultural preservation, social upliftment, and educational advancement. Our purpose is to uphold and share our timeless heritage, extend support to the underserved, and nurture a strong, united, and enlightened society.

Our Dream

We envision a future where our spiritual and cultural values are honored and passed on to the next generations. By making education accessible to all, we strive to empower individuals with the skills and confidence to shape their own success.

Our Impact

Preserving Sacred Spaces

We focus on the construction and development of temples to uphold spiritual and cultural traditions. This includes restoring temple structures, enhancing facilities, and hosting religious and cultural events.

Fostering a Safe and Independent Society

We are committed to building a crime-free and self-sufficient society by encouraging ethical behavior, promoting legal literacy, and supporting vulnerable groups through community-based initiatives.

Combating Child and Bonded Labor

Through public education and direct action, we fight against child and bonded labor. Our work includes victim rescue, rehabilitation, and advocacy for strict enforcement of labor rights and protections.

Challenging Social Injustice

We actively oppose harmful practices such as dowry, caste-based discrimination, and gender bias. Our approach involves awareness drives, legal assistance, and educational outreach to promote equality and justice.

Empowering Through Education

Education remains a key pillar of our mission. We support students with scholarships, enhance learning environments, and provide training for educators—especially to encourage girls’ education and reduce dropout rates.

Promoting a Drug-Free Lifestyle

We address substance abuse through targeted awareness programs, rehabilitation efforts, and community mobilization. In collaboration with health professionals and law enforcement, we aim to build a healthier society.

Shri Devnarayan Mandir Trust is a spiritual and cultural organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage and teachings of Lord Devnarayan. The trust manages the temple activities, organizes religious festivals, and supports various community welfare initiatives. It plays a vital role in maintaining the sanctity of the temple premises, facilitating pilgrim services, and encouraging devotion among followers.
